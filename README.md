# LegalAid

LegalAid is a modern, AI-powered platform designed to streamline legal workflows for law firms and legal professionals. Manage client intake, schedule consultations, search case law, and track casesâ€”all in one place.

## Features

- **Legal Intake**: Collect and organize client and case data automatically for efficient onboarding.
- **Consultation Scheduling**: Clients can schedule and communicate with lawyers easily.
- **Case Law Search Engine**: AI-powered search for relevant statutes, precedents, and legal commentary.
- **Calendar**: Track upcoming dates, deadlines, and case events in one place.
- **Case Tracker & Management**: Timeline for each case, including documents, notes, and assigned lawyers.

## How It Works

1. **Sign Up & Onboard**: Create your account and import your existing cases in minutes.
2. **Automate & Organize**: Let LegalAid handle intake, scheduling, and document management.
3. **Grow & Succeed**: Focus on your clients while LegalAid streamlines your workflow.
